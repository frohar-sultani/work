Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a landlocked country in South-Central Asia with a rich and complex history. Known as a crossroads of many civilizations, it has been influenced by various empires, cultures, and religions over thousands of years. The country is home to diverse ethnic groups, including Pashtuns, Tajiks, Hazaras, and Uzbeks, each contributing their unique traditions and languages. Afghan culture is deeply rooted in Islamic faith and traditional values, with strong emphasis on family, hospitality, poetry, and craftsmanship.
